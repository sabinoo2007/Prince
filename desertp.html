The Desert · Crash Site

You wake in a silent desert

Your airplane is no longer a toy but a broken skeleton of hot metal. In the crushing quiet, every “important” thing you carried feels like a heavy stone in your pocket.

To survive the night, you must decide what to shield from the cold.

What do you protect first?
A) Your Strength. B) Your Attention. C) Your Logic. D) Your Fear.

❌ The Frozen Soul

Loss Condition

You mistook survival for merely existing. By coiling into yourself to save your strength, you froze the very warmth that could have guided you. You survived the night, but you woke up a statue of salt.
