The Desert · Crash Site

You Wake in Silence

As you sit in that profound silence, the sand beside you begins to shift.

It is as if an invisible finger is tracing a memory into the earth.

A gentle voice trembles through the sand: “Tell me… what do you see?”
A) A hat — obviously. B) A boa constrictor digesting an elephant. C) A mountain. Geological formations. D) Abstract art. It means nothing specific.

❌ Adult Blindness

Loss Condition

"You see only surface meaning. The hat is just a hat."

The Little Prince never appears. The desert grows quieter. The sand shifts over your footprints. You are alone — not because you are lost, but because you stopped looking past the surface.

"Grown-ups never understand anything by themselves, and it is tiresome for children to be always explaining things to them."
