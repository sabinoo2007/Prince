Win Condition Achieved

The desert stars flicker and fade. You don't wake elsewhere — you realize you never left your room. The planets were states of your own day. The King is your need for control; the Businessman is your habit of measuring a day by tasks finished; the Geographer is your endless planning for a life you haven't yet dared to live. Now the Little Prince's voice is quiet inside your chest:

"Grown-ups think they live when they are busy. Often, they are only running from silence… while their rose waits for water."

You thought winning the game meant saving the Prince. But the true victory is saving the child inside you who still remembers how to simply be.

The game is over. Your real story begins outside this screen.
