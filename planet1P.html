Planet I · The Sin of Pride

The King

You arrive on a tiny planet where a magnificent throne stands. The King, with a long robe and sparkling crown, sits alone. His eyes scan you carefully. He raises a hand and declares:

"I rule everything. Even you. Even the stars — I order them when to rise."

To pass, you must choose the correct royal gesture. Choose wisely:

A) Tell him he's wrong — kings rule nothing real. B) Demand to be treated as an equal. C) Bow — then ask: "What do you wish of me, Majesty?" D) Ignore him and explore the planet alone.

Planet I · The King · Round 2

He Studies You

"A good king orders only the reasonable. Tell me — should I command the sun to set at midnight?"
A) Yes — you are king, anything is possible. B) "Majesty, a wise king waits until the sun is ready — and orders what will naturally occur." C) "No king has real power over nature."

Hint: Humility is not weakness. It is precision.

👑

❌ Pride Wins

The King banished you. You forgot: true humility is not submission — it is knowing when to yield without losing yourself. You did not show you understood the difference between form and wisdom.

"If I ordered a general to fly from flower to flower, and the general did not obey, which of us would be at fault?"
