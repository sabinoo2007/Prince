❌ The Gilded Cage

Loss Condition: Possession

You confused love with the desire to imprison. By locking the rose under glass, you protected its petals but suffocated its soul. You are left with a beautiful object, but the life within it has withered away.

"Love is not a bird to be caged, but a flower to be watered."

❌ The Spectacle

Loss Condition: External Validation

You reached for the beauty that others admire. In doing so, you handed your heart to the crowd. You are now just another reflection in the man's gallery, waiting for applause that will never satisfy you.

"To be admired is to be alone in a crowd."
