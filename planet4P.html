The Businessman

The Businessman is counting numbers when you arrive. He barely looks up. Click, click, click — his pen races like a heartbeat as he counts stars. To help him answer to this:

"If I have 100 stars and I give you 10% of them, but in doing so, I forget to look at the sky for 10 years... what is the final sum of my wealth?"

You must show him that stars are meant to be seen, not just owned
